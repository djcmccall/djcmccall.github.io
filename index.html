About me

Hello and welcome to the official DJC McCall personal website!! If it was not clear, my name is not "DJC" but just the first three initials of my name. I was initially inspired by Michael Gottlieb, who maintains a website that catalogs his hobbies and projects and I hope to the same here. I am 17 years old and reside in America's one and only "Old Dominion" (Virginia). While I am not the largest fan of writing and english in general, I recently heard a guest speaker at my school say that you should "Write what you know", so I will soon be sharing my opinions on cars, juggling, music, and anything else that interests me. To be clear, THIS IS NOT A BLOG, blogs are the lame passion of subterranean basement dwellers and dogs. enjoy.
